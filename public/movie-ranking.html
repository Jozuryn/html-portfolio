The Best Movies According To Stephen

My Top 3 Movies Of All-Time

Howl's Moving Castle

The movie that made me fall more in love with anime and it's art work.

Lord of the Rings

Big fantasy fan. You can really see how much work and passion was put into the development of characters and the world itself throughout each movie.

The Avengers

Huge Marvel Universe fan. In my opinion, though, the movies have gone a bit down in quality after "End Game".
